About the CUFET Model: As detailed in the accompanying publication A Simple Tool to Estimate Transport GHGs Mitigated from Compact Urban Form (MDPI Applied Sciences, 2026), urban density operates as a robust proxy for the 'D-variables' of compact form. Shifting to compact form shrinks trip lengths and fosters active/transit modal shifts. The impact of density increases is modeled non-linearly, showing significantly higher returns in Small and Medium sized settlements than in Large cities.
